This is a modified copy of this original website. I have added a weight at the bottom of the slinky.

The dynamics of a slinky is well-modeled by a string of n small masses with adjacent masses attached to each other by springs. The bottom mass has an additional mass M.

In the window is shown such a "slinky".

Gravity is pulling it downward, but an invisible hand is holding the top mass to keep the whole thing from falling.

Click the Let Go / Pause button to release the slinky and let it fall.
